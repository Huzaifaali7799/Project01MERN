What is a Self-Backed Loan?

Before borrowing funds and buying crypto with leverage, you need to deposit funds into your Leverix account. The funds you provide are what we call collateral with an option to take a self-backed loan for buying crypto assets.

Suppose you fund your account with $5,000. Using an extension of a self-backed loan from Leverix, you could buy or sell $10,000 worth of BTC/USD on the BTC/USD order book with this account.

Leverage reflects the number of personal funds required to open a position, compared to the size of the position (1:2, 1:5, 1:10, etc.).
